## Commercial Licensing Context

Minelogue uses a server-side dual-license model: the public project remains
available under the AGPL, while the copyright holder may also negotiate separate
commercial license agreements for uses where the AGPL does not fit. The
contribution terms above are meant to keep the public AGPL project available
and avoid blocking commercial licenses for the same Minelogue codebase.
